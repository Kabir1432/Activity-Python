Account Deletion Instructions


If you want to delete your activities for Activity Points ENGG, you can remove them by following these steps:

1. Launch the app: Open the app on your device and Navigate to the Hamburger button: look for an icon that represents in top bar left and side. This is typically denoted by a My profile icon. Tap on it to proceed.



2. Locate the delete account option: Within the account screen, related to account deletion. This option named "Delete Account" Tap on it to proceed. Confirmation screen: After selecting the account deletion option, the app may display a confirmation screen to ensure you want to delete your account. Read the prompt carefully and make sure you understand the consequences of deleting your account..
