Summary of Student Activity Points

We have introduced this page to check the genuinity of Reports and Certificates issued by ACTIVITY POINTS ENGG APPLICATION TEAM. Many students edit the certificate, report and submit the forged copies. To avoid this we have introduced QR Code in the Certificate to check the report & certificate genuinity.

If this page routed from QR Code has the following details, then the report and certificate are considered as genuinely issued by us (Activity Points ENGG Application Team):

  1. Check the URL (it must have the domain as activitypointsengg.com)
  2. Check the Student Details

Professors can contact us on communications@activitypointsengg.com to avail the student's activity log reports or digital proofs.

If the page's URL routed from QR Code does not have the domain activitypointsengg.com and Student details are wrong, then the report and certificate are a forgery, and we, the TEAM of ACTIVITY POINTS ENGG APPLICATION, have not issued the certificate and report. We are not responsible for the activities listed in the report as it is not done under our platform.
